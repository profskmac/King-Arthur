Research Topic: The Knights of the Round Table

Brave Adventurer! Your mission is to unravel the brotherhood of Camelot ‚Äî the legendary Knights of the Round Table. Who were these noble warriors? What code of honor governed their lives? Who were the most famous among them, and what epic quests did they pursue?

Study 2‚Äì3 of the research websites listed below, answer the guiding questions carefully, and document everything on your note-taking handout. Your team's presentation will depend on your bravery and thoroughness!

Part One

The Round Table ‚Äî Brotherhood & Honor

The Round Table Itself

  • 1.What was the Round Table? Why was it built round instead of a traditional rectangular table?
  • 2.Where did the Round Table come from? Who originally owned it, and how did Arthur obtain it?
  • 3.What did the Round Table symbolize? Why was equality important to Arthur's vision of knighthood?
  • 4.What was the "Siege Perilous"? Who was destined to sit in it, and why was it so significant?
  • 5.How many knights could sit at the Round Table?

The Code of Chivalry

  • 6.What was the Code of Chivalry? List and explain at least five of its key values or principles.
  • 7.How did the Code of Chivalry shape the behavior of knights in Arthurian legend?
  • 8.What was a "quest"? Why were quests so important to the knights of the Round Table?
  • 9.What kinds of tasks were knights expected to perform? Give at least three specific examples.

Famous Knights

  • 10.Who was Sir Lancelot? Why was he considered the greatest knight of the Round Table?
  • 11.What secret did Lancelot carry, and how did it ultimately harm Camelot?
  • 12.Who was Sir Galahad? What was his relationship to Lancelot, and why was he considered the purest of all knights?
  • 13.Who was Sir Gawain? What is he most famous for, and what was his relationship to King Arthur?
  • 14.Who was Sir Percival (or Perceval)? What quest is he most associated with?
  • 15.Name two other knights of the Round Table. Briefly describe each one and what they were known for.

Legacy of the Knights

  • 16.How did the fellowship of the Round Table eventually fall apart? What caused its downfall?
  • 17.Why do you think the Knights of the Round Table still capture people's imaginations today? What ideals do they represent?
